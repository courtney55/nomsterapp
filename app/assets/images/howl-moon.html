Howl at the Moon

Howl at the Moon By WhereTraveler ®

Dueling pianists take requests and encourage audience members to sing along and join in the fun. Considering the potent drink offerings, including 86-ounce “buckets of booze,” that’s typically not a problem. M-W 6 pm-2 am, Th-F 5 pm-2 am, Sa 5 pm-3 am, Su 7 pm-2 am.
